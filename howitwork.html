How It Works

Step 1: Choose Your Canvas

Select a canvas that represents the night sky you want to create. You can choose from various beautiful designs!

Step 2: Trace Your Constellation

Use your mouse or finger to trace your desired constellation on the chosen canvas.

Step 3: Save Your Creation

Once you're happy with your traced constellation, enter your name and save your masterpiece!

Step 4: Explore Resources

Explore additional resources and information about constellations and space to learn more.
